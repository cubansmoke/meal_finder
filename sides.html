Sides

Our selection of sides ranges from "little" vibrant bites to "big" comforting platters, making it easy to find the perfect pairing for your meal. If you are looking for a lighter, smaller accent, the Salade Méchouia offers a traditional Tunisian blend of smoky, fire-roasted peppers and tomatoes finished with whole olives, while the Crispy Fan Eggplant delivers a crunchy, panko-coated finger food experience. For a larger, more substantial accompaniment, you can dive into deep comfort with Afghan Borani Banjan, which layers tender baby eggplants in a rich garlic-yogurt sauce, or enjoy a rustic Ratatouille stewed in an olive oil-infused tomato base. Rounding out the hearty options are the timeless French Leek Quiche, baked with a rich egg-and-cream custard in a flaky pastry crust, and a warm Moroccan Chickpea Stew simmered with root vegetables and aromatic Mediterranean spices.
