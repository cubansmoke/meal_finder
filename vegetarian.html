Vegetarian

Vegetarian cooking is an open invitation to celebrate the pure, vibrant flavors of the earth. Far from being just "meals without meat," a great vegetarian dish focuses on building layers of texture and deep, satisfying flavors using whole grains, legumes, fresh produce, and aromatic herbs. By mastering techniques like high-heat roasting to caramelize vegetables, using acids like citrus or vinegar to brighten a dish, and tapping into plant-based umami sources like mushrooms, aged cheeses, or fermented pastes, you can create meals that are incredibly hearty and satisfying. Ultimately, it’s all about letting seasonal ingredients shine and finding joy in the endless, colorful combinations that plant-based cooking offers.
